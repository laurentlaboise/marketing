Affiliate FAQs

How much can I earn?

Example: one Growth Engine client at $649/mo pays about $143/mo at Silver (22%), or one Automation Pro at $1,099 pays about $275/mo at Gold (25%). Stack clients for recurring income; Silver/Gold add new-client bonuses.

Do I deliver the work myself?

No. You introduce and support the client relationship; WordsThatSells delivers the marketing services under scoped packages.

Who should I sell to?

Local SMEs—shops, clinics, hospitality, and service businesses. Laos: Facebook-first, ROI language. Thailand: Growth/Automation with LINE and TikTok awareness. Especially clients who only have social profiles and need a real footprint.

What is “scoped” pricing?

Retainers include hard monthly caps (posts, platforms, content units). They are not unlimited delivery of every catalogue SKU. Overage becomes an add-on or tier upgrade—so commissions stay sustainable.

When do I get paid?

Monthly on the 15th after you reach the $100 minimum payout threshold, via PayPal, bank transfer, or Stripe.
