Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our affiliate program.

What are the commission rates?

We offer a 20% recurring commission for the Standard Plan ($290/month) and 25% recurring for the Professional Plan ($790/month). Performance tiers offer additional bonuses per new client.

How often are commissions paid?

Commissions are paid monthly on the 15th, with a minimum payout threshold of $100. Payments can be processed via PayPal, Bank Transfer, or Stripe.

What kind of support will I receive?

You'll have access to a dedicated Affiliate Manager, 24/7 technical support via tickets and live chat, and a Marketing Specialist for campaign optimization. We also provide a community forum and video training portal.

How long does it take to make my first sale?

Our optimized 30-day onboarding journey is designed to get you ready for sales quickly. Our target for affiliates is to make their first sale within 60 days of onboarding.

What marketing materials are provided?

We provide comprehensive sales collateral (value proposition one-pager, ROI calculator, case study templates), digital assets (email sequences, social media content packs, landing page templates), and educational content (whitepapers, checklists, trend reports).
