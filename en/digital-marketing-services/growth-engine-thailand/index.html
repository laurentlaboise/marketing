Digital Marketing for Thai SMEs
Growth Engine: the SEO agency plan built for Thailand's small businesses

Thai customers search before they buy, chat before they commit, and discover on TikTok. Growth Engine puts your business in all three moments.

If you run an SME in Thailand, your customer's path to you rarely starts at your front door. It starts with a Google search — "dental clinic near me", "office cleaning Bangkok", your product category in Thai or English. It continues in LINE, where Thai customers expect to ask questions, get a quote, and book — often without ever making a phone call. And more and more, it begins on TikTok, where people discover a shop or service they were not even looking for.

Most Thai SMEs know this. The problem is doing something about it every single month. SEO stalls after the website launch. Ads run without anyone tuning them. The LINE Official Account exists but nobody feeds it. Each channel needs steady, unglamorous work — and the owner is busy running the business.

That is the job our Growth Engine plan does. WordsThatSells is headquartered in Vientiane, Laos, and serves SMEs across the region on clearly scoped monthly plans — public prices, hard caps, no "unlimited" fine print. For Thailand, the core is SEO plus ads, with LINE OA and TikTok available as optional add-ons or swaps within your plan's caps.

LINE OA and TikTok — add-ons that fit inside your plan

Thailand is not a one-channel market. Search brings the customer who already wants what you sell; LINE is where Thai customers prefer to talk to a business once they have found it; TikTok is where new customers stumble onto you. A plan that ignores the second and third leaves enquiries on the table.

That is why Growth Engine treats LINE OA and TikTok as optional add-ons or swaps within your plan's caps. If your customers close deals in LINE chats, we can point your SEO and ads at your LINE Official Account and keep its content current. If your product shows well on video, TikTok can take a slot in your channel mix. You are not paying for a bigger plan — you are choosing where the plan's capacity goes.

The caps are the point. Because every plan is scoped with hard limits, a swap is an explicit decision, not scope creep. You always know which channels are covered this month, and what it would take to cover more. The thinking behind the channel choices follows the same playbook we publish in our guides to content marketing strategy and keywords in SEO.

Questions Thai business owners ask us

How much does the Growth Engine plan cost for a Thai SME?

Growth Engine is $649 per month. It is a clearly scoped monthly plan with hard caps: a local SEO cadence, one lead asset per quarter, and ad management, with ad spend billed separately. Digital Footprint at $329/mo and Automation Pro at $1,099/mo sit either side of it. All plans are public on the prices page.

Is ad spend included in the $649/mo plan price?

No. Growth Engine includes ad management — planning, setting up, and running your campaigns — but the ad spend you pay to Google or the social platforms is billed separately. Your plan price stays fixed and your ad budget stays under your control.

Can you manage our LINE Official Account or TikTok?

Yes. For Thailand we run SEO and ads as the core, with LINE OA and TikTok available as optional add-ons or swaps within your plan's caps. You choose the channel mix that matches where your customers are; the plan's scope stays clear either way.

We are not ready for $649/mo. Where should we start?

Start with Digital Footprint at $329/mo: we manage 1–2 platforms and publish 8 posts per month so your business is online and looks professional. When you want a steady flow of enquiries rather than just presence, step up to Growth Engine.

What is a lead asset, and why one per quarter?

A lead asset is a practical piece built to turn visitors into enquiries — a guide, an offer, or landing content your SEO and ads can point to. Growth Engine includes one per quarter, so each asset gets built properly, promoted properly, and measured before the next one starts.
