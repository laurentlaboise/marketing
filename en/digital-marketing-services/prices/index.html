What’s the difference between a retainer and a one-time project?

Retainers (Footprint / Growth / Automation) are monthly capacity—posts, SEO, ops—with a fixed scope. One-time projects (landing pages, sites, content packs) are delivered once with listed revisions. Many clients start with a project, then move onto a retainer.

What does “scoped” monthly mean?

We cap platforms, posts, and deliverables so delivery stays reliable. Need more? We quote an add-on or suggest the next tier—never unlimited work at the lowest price.

Does annual billing change what I get?

Same monthly scope. Annual is 20% off, paid once for the year. Use the toggle above the plans to see the monthly equivalent and year total.

Can affiliates or agencies buy these plans?

Yes. Affiliates earn recurring commission; agencies can white-label delivery. Those tracks have their own pages so this page stays focused on buying for your business.
